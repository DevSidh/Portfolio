03 - Questions and Solutions

IOE-style solved practice

Mathematics

If one root of 3x^2 + 7x + k = 0 is zero, find k.

Answer: 0

If x = 0 is a root, substitute x = 0 into the equation. The remaining value is k, so k must be 0.

The line x + y - 1 = 0 touches y^2 = kx. Find k.

Answer: 4

Write y = 1 - x and substitute in y^2 = kx. Tangency means the quadratic discriminant is zero, giving k = 4.

Physics

Two ice blocks join when pressed together. Why?

Answer: Pressure lowers the melting point of ice.

At the contact surface, pressure causes slight melting; when pressure is released, water refreezes and joins the blocks.

Three equal resistors can make how many different equivalent resistances?

Answer: 4

All series, all parallel, two series plus one parallel branch, and two parallel plus one series are distinct combinations.

Chemistry

In Williamson synthesis, haloalkane is treated with what?

Answer: Sodium alkoxide

Williamson ether synthesis is an SN2 reaction between an alkoxide ion and a suitable primary haloalkane.

A p orbital can accommodate how many electrons?

Answer: 2

Any single orbital can hold a maximum of two electrons with opposite spins.

English

It ___ two weeks since he left the city.

Answer: has been

The sentence links a past action to the present duration, so present perfect is suitable.

What does procrastination mean?

Answer: Delaying work intentionally.

In entrance comprehension and vocabulary questions, know common academic words and their usage.

04 - Chapter-Wise Plan

Build IOE question banks by chapter

Mathematics sequence

Functions -> algebra -> trigonometry -> coordinate geometry -> calculus -> vectors -> probability. Add 20-30 mixed questions per chapter and repeat wrong answers after 48 hours.

Physics sequence

Mechanics -> thermodynamics -> optics -> waves -> electricity -> magnetism -> modern physics. Keep formula sheets beside solved numerical questions.

Chemistry sequence

Physical chemistry numericals first, then inorganic trends, then organic named reactions and functional group conversions.

English sequence

Grammar drills, transformation, vocabulary, phonetics and comprehension. Accuracy matters because negative marking punishes guessing.
